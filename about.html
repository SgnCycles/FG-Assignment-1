About Us

Moving to a new city—or even living in one for years—can sometimes feel surprisingly lonely. Stockholm is full of opportunities, events, and culture, but finding someone to share those experiences with isn't always easy. That's where we come in.

Who We Are

We are two women who know what it's like to look around and think, “This would be so much more fun if I had someone to go with.” Out of that simple thought, this project was born. Our goal is to create a welcoming, supportive community where people—expats, newcomers, and locals alike—can connect with each other and enjoy all that Stockholm has to offer, together.

Why We Started

Whether you've just arrived in Sweden and are still adjusting, or you've lived here your whole life but find yourself wishing for more social connections, you're not alone. Many people share the same struggles: the hesitation to reach out, the challenge of breaking into new social circles, or simply not knowing where to start. We want to make it easier.

What We Do

Our events are all about building bridges. Think concerts, art exhibitions, movie nights, local festivals, day trips, food tastings, outdoor adventures—the list goes on. The idea isn't just to attend an event, but to share the joy of it with people who are equally excited to be there.

Our Community

What makes this space different is that it's not only about the events themselves, but about the sense of belonging that grows around them. We want to foster an environment where people feel safe, seen, and welcome, no matter their background or circumstances. For expats, it can mean finding friends who understand the challenges of starting fresh in a new country. For Swedes, it can mean meeting new people outside of their usual circles and sharing their culture with others. For everyone, it's about creating meaningful connections that last beyond a single outing.
